Keep track of your little angels.

Alleviate stress with our wearable technology that keeps track of groups and lets you know when someone has gone astray. Perfect for taking your little ones out on the town, or even just around the neighborhood. Set boundaries and receive alerts letting you know when they have gone too far.

ABOUT
Haylo is a startup focused on keeping people together. The goal of our product—the Haylo Band—is to provide a low cost way to keep a group of people in physical proximity of one another, ensuring nobody leaves without notifying the others. Our product uses Bluetooth technology, packaged into a comfortable, fashionable wristband, to create a peer-to-peer mesh network. This network is capable of communicating relative location to each member, and will provide notifications amongst members to signal each other when a member leaves the group. The Haylo Band is perfect for any group that wants to stay together. For example, a class of children going on a field trip or a group of friends at a music festival.
